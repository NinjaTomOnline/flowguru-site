FAQ

What to know before FlowGuru opens wider.

Short answers for testers, reviewers, and flow artists who want to know what is real now and what is still ahead.

What is FlowGuru?

FlowGuru is a premium community platform foundation for flow artists. The first release focuses on accounts, profiles, discipline selection, practice prompts, and feedback so the community layer has a solid base.

Is FlowGuru public yet?

No. FlowGuru is preparing for an eventual release and is currently best treated as private early-access software.

Is there an App Store link?

Not yet. This site is ready to become the marketing, support, and privacy surface when the store listing exists.

What platforms are supported?

The current foundation is a Next.js web app with an Express API. Native iPhone and iPad clients are planned as optional future clients that consume the same API.

Does FlowGuru have social feeds or events?

Not in the current build. Social feed, event system, marketplace, uploads beyond profile photos, badges, reputation, and moderation workflows are out of scope for the first foundation.

How is privacy handled?

The current foundation uses first-party onboarding analytics only, does not load third-party tracking scripts, and keeps support/privacy details public for review.
