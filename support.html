Support

Help that keeps the session moving.

FlowGuru is in private staging. Use this page for account help, profile setup questions, tester feedback, and deletion requests.

Email support@flowguru.app

Contact support

Email support@flowguru.app with the email address on your FlowGuru account and a short description of the issue.

Do not send passwords, reset links, verification tokens, private keys, or other secrets in support email.

Common fixes

  • Use the password reset flow if you cannot sign in.
  • Use the verification form if an email link opens in a different browser.
  • Check that profile fields are saved before expecting practice prompts to reflect them.
  • Avatar upload depends on configured staging media storage and is limited to profile photos.

Account deletion

Signed-in users can request deletion from account settings after confirming account email and current password.

You can also email support@flowguru.app from the account email with the subject "Delete my FlowGuru account." The operator confirms the request before removing account and profile records.

Known early-access limits

Payments, tutorial uploads, public feeds, event tools, marketplace listings, reputation systems, and moderation workflows are not active yet.
